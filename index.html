Bridging Relational and Deep Learning


This document provides a quick summary ofthe current efforts made towards bridging relational learning and representation or deep learning. It is made with three objectives in mind:

  • a collection of references addressing the problem of combining relational and deep learning
  • providing a reading guide to a reader new to the field
  • [in progress] providing a summary of the major ideas and the state-of-the-art

Contact

  • If you have comments, suggestions or noticed something I have missed, feel free to contact me at sebastijan.dumancic[AT]cs.kuleuven.be

Acknowledgments

Getting started

  • If you are new to the field of statistical relational learning, check out Introduction to Statstical Relational Learning and Logical and Relational Learning
  • If you are new to deep learning, check out this book

  • International Workshop on Statistical Relational AI
